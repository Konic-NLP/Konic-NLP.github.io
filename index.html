About Me

Sijia Ge is a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Cincinnati, and a member of the Cincy NLP Lab, advised by Prof. Tianyu Jiang.

Previously, Sijia received his Master's degree from the University of Colorado Boulder, where he collaborated with Prof. Martha Palmer on the Knowledge-directed Artificial Intelligence Reasoning Over Schemas (KAIROS) and Uniform Meaning Representation (UMR) projects.

In industry, Sijia worked as an ML/AI Engineer at Rippey.ai , where he specialized in automating systems that process document data and unstructured email text data for the logistics sector.
